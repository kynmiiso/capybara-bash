Meet The Team!

Our group consists of 3 members! Jane Felicia Jonatan, Kimberly Prijadi, and Harsh Shrivastav. Kimberly and Harsh created the game mechanics on Godot, while Jane designed the website and pixel art.



About Our Game | The Capybara Bash

The Capybara Bash is a pixel art platformer game where our capybara, Capy, explores the world in search of their favorite food, Gobi berries. Help Capy traverse numerous platformer levels and different terrains, all while trying to survive and avoid the vicious spike traps. Join Capy on their quest to find the delicious Gobi berries today!
